Biography

Sanjeda Sara Jennifer is a PhD student in Computer Science at UCF AI-AIR lab. Her research interests includes bioinformatics and biomedical AI, especially focusing on the optimization of nanobubble for targeted drug delivery and theraputics. Additionally, she is also working on the microernma and messenger rna for theraputics nd healthcarte AI.. Her research spans ultrasound image augmentation, RNA–mRNA interaction modeling, and medical image analysis, with publications in venues such as the NeurIPS Imageomics Workshop and Heliyon. Before graduate school, she worked as a Software Engineer, AI Engineer and research assistant in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
